Capstone Project Week 4


Team work

We have itemized our future working tasks and organized the Microsoft Azure environment. Everyone was assigned some tasks based on their interest.


We struggled a little bit in our game design development since “how to make our game fun” was a challenging question and that required some experience we didn’t have. We discussed it and we asked for advice from our mentors. The process was challenging, but I am confident we will find the best way for us.

We had our first stand-up-meeting, a quick gloss over what each of us had done and what our plans for the future were. It’s very beneficial to our team work to summarize each member's work and plan for the upcoming tasks together.

My work

This week, I was assigned to work on the Pause Feature.



Once players press the “ESC” button, then it will stop the character from moving (Fixed Update) and the chaos system will be stopped too. The Pause Canvas will be set active.


Lastly, once the players hit the “Back” button, then the game will continue.

After the mentor meeting on Thursday, I received some advice on my code. The Localization System and the Pause Feature parts can be polished so it will be easier to be expanded and to be modified. I plan to work on these parts in the upcoming week.
