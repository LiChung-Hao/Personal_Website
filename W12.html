Capstone Project Week 12


Team work

This week we planned to keep polishing our game. The key features had been completed, so we took advices from our mentors focusing on improving the feedback system to the players, some interesting challenges, and art style consistency.


Since I made the story, lobby, time up, and credit scene, I took the job of making their art style consistent with the game scene. I browsed on Google looking for reference ideas, and drew them using GIMP.

During the production process, I thought about the issue of copyright: If I draw pictures with reference to pictures on the Internet, will there be copyright disputes in commercial use? And from then on, if I use pictures such as ak in my game -47 and other well-known firearms, will there be any doubts about infringing the intellectual property rights of the firearms manufacturer? I discussed these issues with everyone in Discord, and in the end I made a lot of changes to the pictures drawn by Ping. I think this process will be very helpful for our future game development career.
