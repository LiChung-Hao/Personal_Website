Quick Overview Li Chung-Hao

I grew up in an ordinary middle-class family in Taipei, Taiwan. After working in the mobile device industry for a few years, I want to know what kind of career I really want. The answer is to make games that make people cheer. I spend time online learning how to make games by myself. Every time I share my games with my family and friends, I can see that they are excited when they play the games I made. This kind of satisfaction is priceless. The passion for making games brought me to Canada.


Canada has one of the best gaming industries in the world. Many excellent game studios are located in Canadian cities such as Montreal, Toronto and Vancouver. I want to play a role in the gaming industry, and I know I'm on the way there.
