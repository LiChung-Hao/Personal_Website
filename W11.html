Capstone Project Week 11


Team work

This week we planned to add more gameplay content to our game. We listed the tasks to do and people chose which they felt interested in.


We have a simple but completed game. We wanted to make it prettier. Our goals were some art works, game flow improvement, and a credit scene. I kept sending recordings of my work in gif to the team to make sure my teammates knew what I was doing, and they could visualize my artworks and shared their opinions on them. I was very happy about my design in the menu scene. Its art style fits more to our game story and the game scene. My teammates also agreed to it. There were only 20 days left, and finally we could see our work was close to finished.


My work

I finished changing the timer into minutes form very quickly, since all I needed to do was just change the way of calculation.


Then I scripted the function of adding or reducing seconds to the timer, so the teammates who work on gameflow feature can use it as a mechanism.




Then I started working on the credit scene. Since we are not a big team, I can show each teammate one by one with a picture and some text we want in the scene. I had everyone as a game object, and scripted it to be set active on and off one by one.
